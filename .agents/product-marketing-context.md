# OVUM RUSH — product marketing context

_Use with marketing skills (Corey Haines `marketingskills`) for copy, SEO, and growth work._

## Product (one line)

Realtime **men’s lobby + chat** in the browser; optional **neon micro-races** and leaderboards. Cartoon, 18+, entertainment — **not** a clinic and **not** medical advice.

## Primary audience

Adults 18+ interested in men’s wellness conversation, community chat, and light arcade — including “sperm maxing” as a **discussion topic**, not as a product promise.

## Positioning

- **Chat-first:** lobby presence and messaging are the core loop.
- **Races:** optional add-on (queue, short runs).
- **Trust:** clear disclaimers; educational FAQ is **general information** with sources — users must see a clinician for personal decisions.

## Primary conversion goal

Enter lobby → nickname → stay / chat / optional race.

## Channels to emphasize

Organic search (men’s health chat, lobby, sperm maxing discussion), referrals from community, future paid only with compliant copy.

## Words to avoid implying

Diagnosis, treatment guarantees, “we improve your fertility,” replacing a doctor.

## Canonical site URL

Set `NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL` in production for metadata, OG, and sitemap.
